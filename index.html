Open Source Highlights!

Tech Networking

The workshop, titled 'Tech Networking: Harnessing Git, GitHub, LinkedIn, and Twitter for Innovation and Success,' featured distinguished speakers, including Dr. Deepak Thakur and Dr. Monit Kapoor. These esteemed mentors shared valuable insights, covering topics such as data security in open source, file security maintained by major firms, and real-world problem-solving. Their expertise illuminated students on algorithms, resume building, time complexity, and the benefits of a well-crafted GitHub profile. The engaging sessions, led by these accomplished speakers, transformed students' perspectives on development, contributing significantly to the success of the workshop at Chitkara University on October 9, 2023.

Cyber Innovation

The 'Cyber Innovation: Roadmap to Cyber Security Success' workshop at Chitkara University aimed to educate students on cybersecurity. Led by Pawan Gambhir, Open Source Chandigarh's Technical Head, it covered topics like ethical hacking, career paths, and practical applications. Pawan emphasized the importance of cybersecurity in today's interconnected world and highlighted various career paths. The workshop included hands-on sessions on web and network penetration testing, addressing SQL injection vulnerabilities, and introducing tools like Wappalyzer and Google Dorks. Attendees left with insights into cyber security concepts, practical skills, and a structured roadmap for success, including an Interactive OSINT Challenge for location identification.

Boulder blocks

The 'BoulderBlocks: Design Thinking in Blockchain Development' Expert Talk aimed to educate students about blockchain basics and foster learning. Held at Chitkara University on December 4th, 2023, the event covered diverse topics, including cryptocurrency, decentralized finance, and blockchain challenges. Experts from Open Source Chandigarh, such as Parth Gera and Jay Arindum Maity, shared insights, while Aryan Inguz discussed his blockchain hackathon project. The event successfully encouraged active participation, knowledge-sharing, and motivated students to explore blockchain concepts. Participants gained a broader understanding of blockchain's relevance, fostering growth and enthusiasm for learning.
